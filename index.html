What kinds of facts?

We hand-curate each one. Every fact is verified, surprising, and genuinely delightful.

🧪 Science

A teaspoon of a neutron star would weigh about 10 million tons — roughly the same as a mountain.

🏛️ History

Cleopatra lived closer in time to the moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid of Giza.

🐙 Animals

Octopuses have three hearts, blue blood, and nine brains — one central brain and one mini-brain in each arm.

🌍 Geography

There are more possible iterations of a game of chess than there are atoms in the observable universe.

🗣️ Language

"Petrichor" is the word for the distinctive smell of rain on dry earth — from Greek petra (stone) and ichor (the fluid in gods' veins).

🧠 Psychology

The "doorway effect" is real — walking through a doorway causes the brain to file away recent memories, making you forget why you came in.

Simple answers to simple questions

Do I need to download anything?
Nope. Facts arrive as plain text messages to your phone. If you can receive a text, you're good to go.
What time do facts arrive?
Every morning between 8–9 AM in your local time zone. A nice way to start the day.
How do you choose the facts?
Our small editorial team reviews and curates each fact by hand. We prioritize accuracy, wonder, and variety across topics.
Where exactly does the money go?
We donate 100% of net subscription revenue to vetted children's literacy nonprofits and publish a transparency report every quarter.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — text STOP at any time and you'll be unsubscribed instantly, no questions asked.
